About MM GRINDING MILL

MM Grinding Mill is a trusted and modern manufacturing unit specializing in the production and supply of high-quality Calcium Carbonate Powder. Located in the heart of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, we are strategically positioned to cater to industries across Pakistan with efficiency and reliability.

Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art grinding technology and advanced quality control systems, enabling us to deliver products that meet stringent national and international standards. By focusing on precision and consistency, we ensure that our Calcium Carbonate powders maintain superior purity, uniform particle size distribution, and optimum performance for a wide range of industrial applications.

Our Commitment

  • Consistent Product Quality through strict quality checks at every stage of production.
  • Timely Deliveries to clients across Pakistan.
  • Competitive & Transparent Pricing ensuring fair value.
  • Sustainable Practices for environment and community.
  • Long-Term Partnerships built on trust, integrity, and reliability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower industries across Pakistan by supplying Calcium Carbonate powders that comply with international benchmarks of quality, purity, and performance. We aim to be the preferred partner for businesses in sectors including Paints & Coatings, Plastics, Paper & Packaging, Rubber, Adhesives, Pharmaceuticals, Food, and Construction.

Production Capacity & Packaging

  • Monthly Production Capacity: 3,000 Metric Tons
  • Packaging Options: 50 kg bags, 25 kg bags, Jumbo Bags, and Custom Packaging
