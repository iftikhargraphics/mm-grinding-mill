Material Safety Data – Calcium Carbonate

Identification of Substance

Product Name: Calcium Carbonate (Limestone Powder)
Recommended Uses: Filler and extender in rubber, plastics, and paints; opacifying and coating agent in papers; putty; toothpaste; antacid; animal nutrition; and metallurgical flux.

Hazard Identification

Not classified as hazardous under GHS. Non-flammable and non-toxic under normal handling conditions.

First Aid Measures

  • Inhalation: Move person to fresh air; provide oxygen if needed.
  • Skin Contact: Wash with soap and water for 15-20 minutes.
  • Eye Contact: Rinse with clean water for 15-20 minutes; seek medical attention if irritation persists.
  • Ingestion: Rinse mouth; do not induce vomiting; seek medical help if discomfort continues.

Handling & Storage

Avoid breathing dust; use in a well-ventilated area. Keep containers tightly closed in a cool, dry place away from acids and oxidizing agents.

Exposure Controls / Personal Protection

  • Use local exhaust ventilation to reduce airborne dust.
  • Wear dust respirator, protective gloves, lab coat, and safety goggles.

Physical & Chemical Properties

  • Appearance: White powder
  • Odor: Odorless
  • Melting Point: 825 °C
  • Specific Gravity: 2.8 g/cm³
  • Solubility: Very slightly soluble in cold water; soluble in dilute acid

Stability & Reactivity

Stable under normal conditions. Avoid contact with strong acids and oxidizing agents. Non-corrosive and non-polymerizing.

Ecological & Disposal Information

Non-toxic and non-hazardous to the environment. Dispose of in compliance with local regulations. Empty containers should not be used for food or water storage.

This information is provided in good faith as a guide to safe handling and use of Calcium Carbonate products from MM Grinding Mill.
